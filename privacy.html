Privacy Policy – findME App

Effective Date: March 2026

1. Introduction

Radikal Solutions (“we,” “our,” “us”) operates the findME emergency alert application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email, and emergency contacts you provide.
  • Device Information: Android device ID, app version, and system logs.
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates when you trigger an emergency alert.

3. How We Use Information

  • To send emergency alerts to your chosen contacts.
  • To improve app performance and reliability.
  • To comply with legal obligations if required.

4. Sharing of Information

  • Emergency Contacts: Your location and alert message are shared only with the contacts you configure.
  • Third‑Party Services: We use providers like Firebase, Twilio, and Pipedream to deliver alerts.
  • We do not sell or rent your personal information.

5. Data Security

We use encryption and secure storage (SharedPreferences, Firestore) to protect your data. While no system is 100% secure, we take reasonable steps to safeguard your information.

6. Your Rights

  • You may update or delete your emergency contacts at any time.
  • You may uninstall the app to stop all data collection.
  • For questions, contact us at findme@radilkalsolutions.co.za.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on our website.
